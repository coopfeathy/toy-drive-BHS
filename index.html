There is a Toy Drive being held to benefit the children at Arkansas Children's Hospital-Northwest. The Toy Drive is sponsored by Farm Bureau in conjunction with the Bentonville Basketball Team. All toys will be gathered throughout the week and brought together at the first home game of the season against Greenwood High School. Anything you can give would be appreciated greatly. Please keep in mind that the toys are for kids ages 0 to 18 and not just little children.



"At Arkansas Children’s, we not only make sick and injured kids better today, we also care for the entire family, with such services as social work, child life, education and pastoral care. Many of these needs are met through the generosity of donors like you. Your gifts of new toys, handmade items and other new gifts go a long way toward supporting families and brightening a hospital stay for a patient. Because the health and safety of our patients is our #1 concern, we’ve developed several wish lists that meet our infection control standards".
